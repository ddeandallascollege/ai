Instructional Philosophy

As a professor at Dallas College, my mission is to prepare students not just with technical domain skills, but with the cognitive habits necessary to survive and thrive in a hyper-connected economy.

My modern coursework integrates critical reflective prompts, behavioral experiments, and peer workshops designed to rebuild attention stamina. By examining digital tools with a mindful eye, students learn to maintain control over their focus, creative depth, and digital wellness.
